Abstract: Currently there is no radially accessible information on the material properties of transition metal nitrides. This database was created to make the information publicly accessible by researchers in the field as well as industry professionals. This paper describes the creation and operation of the database that was created.

Index Terms: Transition metal nitrides, database creation.
