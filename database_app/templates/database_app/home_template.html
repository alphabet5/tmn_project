    Structure
    This refers to the crystal structure that the transition metal was analysed in.



    Metal
    This refers to the transition metal that is used in the nitrate.



    Pugh's ratio
    Pugh's ratio (k) as k=G/B (B = Bulk Modulus and G = Hill's arithmetic mean) The units for Pugh's ratio are GPa.




    Vickers Hardness
    An estimation of Vickers Hardness with the units in GPa.





    Lattice Constant
    The lattice constant refers to the physical dimensions of a crystal lattice. For cubic structures there is only one value. The units for Lattice constant are in angstroms.



    Bulk Modulus
    Bulk modulus is a substance's resistance to uniform compression. Units are given in GPa.





    Cauchy Pressure
    Cauchy Pressure Pc = C12-C44






    Poissons Ratio
    Poissons ratio is the negitave ratio of transverse to axial strain. No units.



    Experimental
    This field defines whether the result is an experimental or computational result.
    Stable
    If the structure is stable this field will be true.

    Method
    This shows whether pseudo potential or PAW was used for the calculation.


    Potential
    This shows what method of approximation was used in density functional theory. Local Density Approximation, General Gradient Approximation, etc.


    Reference DOI
    This is the reference DOI of the paper that the results were obtained from.
